  • Interested in being a ./CS Officer?
    We are looking to expand our officer team for the next year! If you are interested in helping out (and will be at Ohlone next year), email secretary@dot-slash-cs.club for more information!

    We are looking forward to attending SiliconHacks 2017. Everyone is wecome to join and there is no skill level required. If you are interested in attending, please use the hyperlink to go to the SiliconHacks 2017 webpage and fill out all the required forms.
  • We have officially begun club projects.
    It's not too late to join our club project of building a web application that will be able to create various possible schedules for your classes at Ohlone College. Input the classes you are going to take and we will output possible schedules that won't have conflicts. Join us today! Come to our meeting on Friday to join! If you still would like to join but are unable to make meetings, please contact us at cin@dot-slash-cs.club and we will work with you to get you involved!
  • STEM Outreach: Hidden Figures Movie Screening
    We are hosting a movie screening of the Oscar Nominated movie Hidden Figures on May 5th! It will be free and there will be food and drinks available for discounted prices for Ohlone Students.
